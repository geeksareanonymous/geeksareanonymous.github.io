Who We Are

We are Anonymous Geeks, we help people to attend hackathons.

Anonymous Geeks is an initiative for hackers who want to attend hackathons but due to financial problems they can't attend. So simply we help them by covering there travel!

How We Work

Application

Hackers submit the application form after being accepted in there favourite hackathon. Application only opens when we have enough funds.

Eligibility

After recieving application, we will contact the hackathon organizer if they are providing reimbursement. The hacker will be only eligible if the hackathon is providing less than 75% of your total cost. Visa fees is not included.

Results

Hackers who submitted application result within few bussiness days after submiting application.

Represent!

Accepted hackers will have to represent Anonymous Geeks as there team name and wear Anonymous Geek t-shirt if shipped before your hackathon.
